Privacy Policy

OENOD LIVE has an integrated mediasoup server. Routing is a multiparty topology, where each participant sends its media to this server and receives all other's media from it. Thanks to the SFU architecture, it allows having many users connected in the same room. The media streams are encrypted using Secure Real-time Transport Protocol (SRTP).

If you record a meeting, the recording of the meeting is temporarily stored on Your browser Blob, then will be downloaded on Your PC/Mobile Device.

We use Umami to track aggregated usage statistics in order to improve our service. The maker of OENOD LIVE has no intention of using personally or selling any of the above-mentioned data.
